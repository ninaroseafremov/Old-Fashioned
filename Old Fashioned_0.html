Old Fashioned Cocktail

Ever wanted to learn how to make an Old Fashioned?

An Old Fashioned is a popular whiskey based cocktail that adults of all ages can enjoy whether they are with friends at the bar or relaxing alone on a weekend night at home. Due to the nature of the Covid-19 pandemic, many do not feel safe going out to bars and restaurants, but that does not mean that they should be deprived of a classic cocktail.
