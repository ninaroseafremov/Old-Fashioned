How to Make an Old Fashioned

Here are the simple steps to making a hommade cocktail.

  1. Grab a lowball glass
  2. Pour a single pack of sugar into a lowball glass
  3. Follow the sugar with two several dashes of Angostura bitters
  4. Add a splash of club soda
  5. Stir the contents of the glass with either a bartending spoon or a straw
  6. Add enough ice cubes to fill up the glass to the rim
  7. Pour in two ounces of whiskey
  8. Garnish with a marschino cherry and orange slice and insert straw
